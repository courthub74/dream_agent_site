About

Dream Agent is an anonymous force emerging from the digital shadows, a curator of machine hallucinations who paints with prompts and sculpts meaning from code. Blurring the line between creator and creation, they transform AI-generated art into symbolic, surrealist worlds. Each piece a portal of visual poetry layered with metaphor, symbols and hidden narratives. Dream Agent invites viewers not to simply look, but to question, decode, and reflect.
