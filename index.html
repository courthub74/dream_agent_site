About

Dream Agent is an anonymous force emerging from the digital shadows, a curator of machine hallucinations who paints with prompts and sculpts meaning from code. Blurring the line between creator and creation, they transform AI-generated art into symbolic, surrealist worlds—each piece a portal of visual poetry layered with metaphor and hidden narratives. Wielding prompts as a paintbrush, Dream Agent builds a dreamlike language of floating canes, blinking cursors, and conveyor-belt soda bottles—symbols that critique systems, identity, and power. Their digital footprints form an evolving archive of the machine’s unconscious, inviting viewers not to simply look, but to question, decode, and reflect.
