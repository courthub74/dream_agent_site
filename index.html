Who is Dream Agent?

Emerging from the shadows of the digital realm, Dream Agent exists not as a face, but as a surrealist force. A curator of machine hallucinations who paints with prompts and sculpts meaning from code. Operating anonymously across the web, Dream Agent blurs the line between creator and creation, transforming AI-generated art into deeply symbolic, surrealist worlds. Their work speaks in riddles and symbols, conjuring visual poetry from data, algorithms, and imagination. Each piece is less a picture and more a portal—one that invites viewers to question, decode, and reflect.
